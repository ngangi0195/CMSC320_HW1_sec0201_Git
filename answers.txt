Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We can use Git to track changes over time, collaborate without overwriting, manage versions, and back up work.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to the healthcare industry. Hospitals can use models to predict disease in patients based on a multitude of factors and trends.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines the full contents of two branches together whereas rebasing applies 
one branch's changes on top of another, applying changes more linearly.
